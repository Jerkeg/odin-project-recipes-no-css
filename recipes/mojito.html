Mojito

A refreshing drink for a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • Rum
  • Mint
  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Club soda

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Place mint leaves and a lime into a sturdy glass.
    Use a muddler and crush to release mint oilts and lime juice
  3. Add remaining lime wedges and 2 tablespoons sugar. Muddle again.
    Do not strain the mixture.
  4. Fill the glass almost to top with ice
  5. Pour in rum and fill the glass with club soda.
  6. Stir, taste and add sugar if neccesary.
