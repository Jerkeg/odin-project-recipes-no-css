Chicken Piccata

These chicken piccatas are to be made in one pan with lemon juice, capers, white wine and butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 shallots
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bread crumbs
  • Finely minced parsley
  • Large egg
  • Ground chicken
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • White wine
  • Lemons
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Add shallots to large bowl. Stir in Parmesan cheese, add bread crumbs parsley and egg.
    Add ground chicken, season with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
    Mix gently together until everything is well incorporated.
  2. Add remaining bread crumbs to a work surface or shallow dish.
    Form chicken mixture into 12 meatballs, rolling each in bread crumbs to coat and help shape.
  3. Heat oil in large deep skillet over medium to high heat.
    Add meatballs to skillet and cook, turn them occasionally until they are bron on all sides.
    Remove meatballs from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add remaining shallot to the skillet and cook stirring constantly for a minute.
    Pour in wine and stock and bring to a boil.
    Return meatballs to skillet, reduce heat and let simmet until liquid is reduced by half.
    Stir as needed.
  5. Once sauce is reduced, move meatballs to one side of the skillet and turn the heat to the lowst.
    Whisk in lemon juice and cold butter. Stir constantly.
    Stir in parsley and remove from heat.
    Serve immediately.
